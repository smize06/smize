Why I Chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth for its strong business program, welcoming campus community, and the beautiful location near Lake Superior. UMD offers the perfect balance of academic rigor and personal growth opportunities.

What I Like About Duluth

Duluth is a vibrant city with a unique blend of nature and culture. I love exploring the outdoors, enjoying the lake views, and experiencing the local food and music scene.

Campus Life & Fraternity Involvement

Being an officer in Phi Kappa Psi has given me leadership experience and lifelong friendships. I enjoy participating in campus events, volunteering, and making the most of my college years.
